The Best Movies according to Pratik

My top 3 Movies of all-time

12 Angry Men

An absolute masterpiece. Classical Drama at it's peak, It shows in order to make a great movie you don't need expensive budget


The Dark Knight Returns

This is my favourite animation movie of all time. As an already batman fan, I thought nothing could make me even more fan of this character. But this movie made me believe why Batman is the Greatest superhero of all time


Prisoners

In my opinion this movie is still underrated, the first time I watched it I didn't realize what was so special about it. Seemed like a straightforward movie with a simple plain plot. But then when I connected the dots, made 2+2=4 and realized the proper analogy of the movie, My mind was just blown away
